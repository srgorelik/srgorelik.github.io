I am a masters student in the Department of Geography at the University of Idaho. I research tree mortality related to climate change in the piñon-juniper woodlands of northern New Mexico using a combination of satellite imagery, statistical modeling, and computer programming.


I have previously worked for the NASA DEVELOP National Program, Nextdoor.com, the Golden Gate National Parks Conservancy, and the University of California, Santa Barbara on projects involving:

  • remote sensing and geographic information systems
  • natural resource management and conservation
  • cartographic design and geovisualization
  • urban forestry
