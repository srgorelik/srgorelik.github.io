Amazon Forest Carbon Dynamics

A recently published study I co-authored. We reported changes in forest carbon across the Amazon basin and our findings indicate that increased protection of indigenous land rights and better monitoring and ...management of emissions from forest degradation are needed if Amazon basin countries are to achieve their commitments under the Paris Agreement.

Drought-Induced Tree Mortality

In the early 2000s, severe drought and beetle outbreaks brought on by climate change led to extensive piñon pine mortality in northern New Mexico. Mapping piñon mortality is critical for understanding and monitoring ...impacts to ecosystem services. Here, in my Master's thesis, I used Landsat imagery, finer-resolution reference data, and statistical modeling to predict absolute within-pixel percent tree mortality (PTM). I found that a balanced random forest model had the highest accuracy. I applied the model to a time series and assessed spatial and temporal patterns of the estimated tree mortality. This work demonstrates the capability of mapping drought-induced PTM using Landsat time series imagery.
